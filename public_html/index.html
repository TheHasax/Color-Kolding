IBA have arranged a wonderful event called “Color Kolding”, which is happening on 10. June 2017. It is going to be a colorful day with many sports activities and funny tasks, and then in there will be a huge competition with big prizes! The actual day is about painting Kolding! That means, that during the day of the event, there will be different arrangements in different areas in Kolding. There are going to be great activities for both kids and adults, and then there is also tasty food.

1 2 3 4 5 6

Paintball for adults (16+)

Paintball is one of the coolest activities that give you an amazing experience. The idea is that you have to shoot “paintballs” which are produced by eco-friendly plastic and paint. You can freely haunt and shoot your opponent, with these fun and colourful paintballs!

Paintball for kids (10+)

Are you under 16, and still want to play paintball? Then don’t you worry! ColorKolding is offering paintball for kids, where the balls are softer and made of balloons with paint in them. Despite that they are also going to wear a security uniform.

Color-run at Kolding Slotssøen

You don’t need to go all the way to India to get a flavour of the big Holi-festival! Just join us at “Slotssøen” and take a run around the lake, while there will be thrown wonderful colors at you. We promise you an amazing experience! All the colors that we a going to use are 100% eco-friendly.

Volley splat

Can you feel that summer is getting closer? Or can you feel the will desire to play some volley? We have actually arranged “Volly splat”, which has same rules as traditional Volleyball, but the in a colourful sand which will make it much more fun!

Color-shot

If you like to paint or to shoot, then this is something for you! Color-shot. The idea is that you get a normal bow and arrow, which you dip, in some paint and then shoot at a giant canvas. And then the game is up to you, feel free to create an awesome masterpiece.

Food-stand

An event without food? Impossible! We are offering tasty and creative food that doesn’t just taste good but also looks incredibly unique! We have everything from blue sausages to green buns!
